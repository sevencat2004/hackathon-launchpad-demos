## Candidate Product

Working title: Zero Stack BountyOps.

Build an AI operations room for bounty and grant execution. A user enters a GitHub issue,
grant program, or hackathon link; the app produces:

- payout collectability score
- duplicate-competition scan
- account/KYC/payment risk checklist
- implementation plan
- deployable demo packet
- user-action handoff

## Why This Can Win

Most hackathon "agent" demos stop at chat. This product produces operational artifacts:
rankings, risk reasons, submission packets, and audit trails. It can demonstrate real value
using the user's existing bounty workflow without exposing private data.
